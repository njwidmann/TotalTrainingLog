About Total Training Log

Total Training Log is a free service that aims to allow all athletes, ranging from marathon runners to MMA
fighters, to easily record a complete and total log of their training.

To accomplish this goal, Total Training Log provides a wide range of preset exercises with an easy option
to painlessly add more. This way, whether you need to keep track of miles ran, weights lifted, balls thrown,
or hot-dogs eaten, there is no better way than by using Total Training Log.
